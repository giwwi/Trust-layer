Trust Layer

Paste an analytical text. Get a first reading you can check and edit.

Trust Layer is a bounded prototype for analytical texts: research drafts, policy memos, white papers, analytical essays, and book proposals. It helps identify main claims, what needs attention, and the next review step before deeper human checking.

  • Upload or paste an analytical text.
  • Get a provisional AI first reading.
  • Check source traces and original text.
  • Edit the result as a human reviewer.
  • Stop, continue, or escalate.
This is not a truth engine, not an AI detector, and not expert review. Open this page in a browser with JavaScript enabled to use the live demo. For a non-JavaScript map of the product, open the static overview.
